About The Unit

About The Unit

The Duvdevan Unit was established in June 1986, with the understanding that a specific and intelligent warfare method needed to be developed to deal with Judea and Samaria's security incidents, where there is a dense civilian population. The Duvdevan Unit is an elite unit, professional in its field, and dedicated to fighting in various field descriptors. The Unit works to prevent hostile terrorist activity and operates openly and disguised among a local Arab population. The Unit’s soldiers are engaged in ongoing operational activities, carry out nocturnal arrests, seize perpetrators, create ambushes, and many undercover operations.

Over the years, The Duvdevan Unit has been recognized for its intensive and important work and has received numerous accolades and medals. "For carrying out a large number of activities with courage and high operational level, while taking the initiative and discovering creativity."

Shaul Mofaz, 2001 – In 2001, the Unit was awarded the Prime Minister's Award for Special Activity by Prime Minister Ariel Sharon, which was, in fact, the first time that a military unit won such an award. Also, the unit holds three citations from the Chiff Of Staff. During the rise in terrorism that began in 2015, the Unit’s activity was important and necessary in the daily struggle against terror. Most of the significant arrests during this period are credited to the Unit. As a result of the intense and important activity in this wave of terror, the IDF Chief of Staff awarded the unit a heroic medal.

About the Foundation

The Duvdevan Foundation was established in 1992 by a bereaved father who lost his son in the Duvdevan Unit. This Foundation has been recognized as a legal charity by federal law and is registered as “Friends of Duvdevan" (Tax ID – 83-0860160). We are here to directly impact the lives of soldiers & veterans of Duvdevan and help them In their transition from intense military service to civilian life.

We have taken it upon ourselves to give back to these brave young men and women who chose to risk their lives for the State of Israel and Its people. The Duvdevan Foundation is dedicated to the Zionist values of honesty, comradeship, and social involvement. The "Compounded Impact" – Our agenda is to empower Israeli society by empowering our graduates. This unique circle of giving allows our supporters to participate in making a direct impact on Society itself. The Foundation’s activities:

Education – Every year out of 120 supported scholarship recipients, we offer 40 new ones. Leadership& Empowerment – A Unique Mentoring program. Professional Career Guidance. Support for post-trauma victims, Support for Corona victims.

Commemoration – Financial and Emotional Support to our bereaved families as well as our lone soldiers.
Distribution of food baskets and ongoing assistance throughout the year.
